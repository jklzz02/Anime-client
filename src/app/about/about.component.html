About AnimeHub

Welcome to AnimeHub — a passion project crafted with love by a solo developer who’s deeply in love with anime and clean web experiences. This is my hobby, my playground, and my way to give back to the community.

Why AnimeHub?

AnimeHub was born out of my frustration with the clutter and complexity of existing anime tracking sites. I wanted something fast, simple, and beautiful — a place where fans can easily explore, discover, and discuss their favorite shows without distractions.

What does AnimeHub offer?

AnimeHub not only offers content browsing, the possibility of making watchlists and discussing content with other fans, it also provides a fully free and open source with no tracking and no need of authentication.
